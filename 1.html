KAYANGAN LAKE

  • <
  • >

One of the most stunning and memorable beaches in all of the Philippines is at Kayangan Lake on the island of Coron. For the most well-rounded aquatic experience, from swimming to scenery to cliff diving, this is your beach.

Coron is a popular island for shipwreck diving and viewing marine life and its coral gardens, but Kayangan Lake is a tranquil base and a good place to mix-up the activity with relaxation. The clear water and views are nothing short of astounding. Climb the stairs of the overlook for the best aerial view or book a boat tour to see the scenery.
