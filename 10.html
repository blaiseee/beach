HONDA BAY

  • <
  • >

Honda Bay in Puerto Princesa on Palawan Island is a popular beach for snorkeling, swimming, and relaxing by the water. Island hopping tours are popular out of Honda Bay and a great way to experience several beaches while dining on local fare. The beach has plenty of water sports, like jet skiing and wakeboarding.

You can daydream by the coast or explore coral reefs - whatever fits your mood. Enjoy a beachside lunch of fresh seafood from one of the local vendors. It is best to stay at a resort like Daluyon Beach and Mountain Resort in Puerto Princesa or the Canvas Boutique Hotel, and catch a ferry to Honda Bay for a day trip. If you plan an island-hopping tour, book it through a larger resort.
