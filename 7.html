ANGUIB BEACH

  • <
  • >

To experience the untouched natural beauty of the Philippines, Anguib Beach in the Santa Ana region fits the bill. The white-sand beach is beautiful, but the mangrove trees that surround it add a tropical ambiance.

You can rent kayaks and snorkeling gear beachside, which is convenient. The scenery is filled with volcanic rocks and diverse flora and fauna. Most of the lodging in the area is comprised of small independent cottages or small resorts like the Jotay Resort.

Be mindful of times for high tide and low tide, especially if you are traveling with children as drop-offs in the water can be quite deep. The historical town of Santa Ana is worth a visit while you are in the area.
